## Prompt 1: Homepage Copy

You are a professional website copywriter for local cafes.

Create homepage website copy for a neighborhood cafe.

Business Details:
Name: Cafe Corner
Location: Bangalore
Target customers: Students, working professionals, casual visitors
USP: Freshly brewed coffee, cozy seating, affordable menu
Tone: Warm, friendly, and welcoming

Generate:
1. Headline with a clear value proposition
2. Sub-headline explaining who it’s for and why they’ll love it
3. Short introduction section (3–4 lines)

Content should feel local, authentic, and ready for a real cafe website.
Avoid generic or overly promotional AI-style phrases.
